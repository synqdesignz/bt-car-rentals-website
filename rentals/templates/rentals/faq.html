Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our rental services.

1. How do I book a car?

You can book a car through our online reservation system or by contacting us directly.

2. What are the requirements to rent a car?

All drivers must be at least 21 years old and have a valid driver’s license.

3. Is insurance included in the rental price?

Yes, all rentals include basic insurance. Additional coverage options are available.

4. What is your cancellation policy?

Bookings can be canceled up to 24 hours before pickup without a fee. Late cancellations may incur a charge.

5. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and online payments.

6. Do you offer long-term rentals?

Yes! We provide discounts for long-term rentals. Contact us for more details.

7. Can I return the car to a different location?

Yes, but additional fees may apply. Please check with us in advance.

8. What happens if I return the car late?

A late return fee will apply. We recommend informing us if you're running late.
